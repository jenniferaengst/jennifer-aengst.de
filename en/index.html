Services I offer

Senior Digital Project Management

Complex digital projects with demanding stake­holder structures and intricate depen­dencies require clear owner­ship, focused communi­cation and reliable delivery. I take end-to-end responsi­bility – from timeline and budget to risks and depen­dencies – ensuring priorities are clear and progress remains visible throughout.

Project Delivery Stabilisation

Projects under pressure often share the same root causes: unclear priorities, frag­mented communi­cation and shifting require­ments. I identify the struc­tural blockers, realign teams and stake­holders, and re-establish a realistic delivery setup – restoring clear priorities, trans­parent communi­cation and early visibility of risks.

PM Workflow & Process Optimisation

An outside perspective reveals what those closest to the work cannot easily see. I analyse project work­flows, communi­cation patterns and PM structures, identify ineffi­ciencies and sources of rework, and develop targeted measures – tailored to the specific context of the project – to strengthen coordi­nation and optimise processes across teams.

Tool Selection & Introduction

New tools only deliver value if adoption is sustain­able. I manage the selection and intro­duction of tools – including AI-supported solutions – that enhance communi­cation, documen­tation and workflow efficiency, starting with a thorough require­ments analysis, followed by a structured, phased rollout and tailored training and documentation.
