Services I can offer you

Project Management

The overall success of a project depends largely on the avail­ability and qualifi­cation of the Project Manager. In addition to metho­dological knowledge and valuable experience, the right soft skills are essential. If you currently do not have the required resources, I will be happy to support you.

Consulting

Whether introducing or optimizing project management tools and processes - I will be happy to advise you, your teams and involved parties with the goal of increasing efficiency. Further­more, I analyze cross-functional challenges, evaluate possible solutions and create transformation roadmaps.

Coaching & Mentoring

Changes and novel situations can result in uncertainty and scepticism. I will be happy to work with your teams through essential change processes and act as a mentor for junior project managers helping them to develop the appropriate metho­dological and inter­personal approach.

Key Account Management

Excellent key account management is charac­terised by the client feeling informed, heard and understood at all times. At the same time, require­ments and deliv­erables are clearly defined and expec­tations are set realis­tically. I will be happy to manage your most important clients with a keen sense for their individual needs.
